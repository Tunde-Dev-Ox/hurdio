What is Hurdio?

Hurdio is an online music publication platform covering Afrobeats, Hip-hop, R&B, Jazz, Soul, Gospel, Pop, Rock, film music, classical, and other music genres. We are providing a platform to get reviews, interviews, podcasts, trending news, and more about the music industry and your favourite artists. Dive deep into the vibrant world of music with Hurdio, the online platform dedicated to everything that makes music great - from playlists, artists mining, playlist curating, festivals, to online concerts. We connect you with the artists, the industry, and the stories that fuel your favorite tunes. Here are some of the things you can expect at Hurdio:

Reviews & Ratings

Get access to reviews and ratings of the latest music releases. We cover everything from albums, singles, and EPs.

Curated Playlists

We offer pre-made cross-platform playlists based on genres, moods, activities, or themes, curated by our editors or leveraging algorithms.

In-Depth Artist Profiles

Get to know your favorite artists better with our in-depth artist profiles. We cover everything from their music, personal life, and more.

News and Latest trend

Stay up to date with the latest music news and trends. We cover everything from new releases, music festivals, and more.

Music Distribution & Promotion

We offer music distribution and promotion services for artists and record labels. Get your music heard by the right audience.

Music Concerts

Get access to exclusive online concerts and music festivals. We offer a platform for artists to perform and connect with their fans. Buy tickets to your favorite concerts.

"I want to create a platform that goes beyond simply listening to music. I want to connect people with the artists, the industry, and the stories that fuel their favorite tunes. I want the unheard artist to be heard same way as the established artistes. This is what Hurdio is all about".

Joseph Tunde Founder, Hurdio
